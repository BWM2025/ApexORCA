# Example Short Mandates — Operations Pod (Copy & Paste)

Use these in WhatsApp (or your chat interface) to get immediate wins with your Operations-Lead. One line = one mandate.

---

## Ops brief

- **Run HEARTBEAT and give me the ops brief.**
- **Summarize fulfillment status and open support tickets.**
- **What are the top 3 operational risks right now?**

---

## Fulfillment & support

- **List any delayed or failed fulfillments from the last 7 days and what you did about them.**
- **Summarize support ticket volume and resolution rate this week.**
- **Audit last week’s orders for any mismatches; report and fix.**

---

## Revenue & alerts

- **Any checkout or payment anomalies in the last 24 hours?**
- **Give me a one-line revenue and fulfillment health check.**
- **What would you escalate to me right now if you had to choose one thing?**

---

## One-off checks

- **What’s one operational improvement you’re ready to implement this week?**
- **Summarize customer feedback trends and one change you’re making.**
- **Give me the nightly ops summary in 5 bullets.**

---

Short mandates only. The pod runs the rest.
