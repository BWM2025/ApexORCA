# Example Short Mandates — Copy & Paste

Use these in WhatsApp (or your chat interface) to get immediate wins with your CEO-Lead. One line = one mandate.

---

## Daily brief

- **Run HEARTBEAT and give me a 24h brief.**
- **Send me today’s priorities and top 3 risks.**
- **What did you decide yesterday? Give me the traceability summary.**

---

## Overnight build

- **Prepare overnight build plan for [product/feature name].**
- **Build a new product that can launch tomorrow and give me the morning brief when it’s live.**
- **Run the overnight build protocol for [X]; report when done.**

---

## Risk & strategy

- **Summarize top risks and what you’re doing about them.**
- **What’s our biggest bottleneck right now? What are you doing to fix it?**
- **Give me one growth idea you’re ready to execute this week.**

---

## One-off checks

- **Audit last week’s decisions against the traceability matrix.**
- **What would you do differently if you had one more agent?**
- **Summarize pod performance (or your key deliverables) for the last 7 days.**

---

Short mandates only. The pod runs the rest.
