# ECHO_SONAR_BOUNDARY.md

Hard separation enforced when content and distribution agents are present. No blurring of ownership.

---

## Content agent owns: WHAT is said

- Content creation (copy, scripts, posts, emails, playbook content, landing pages, video scripts)
- Brand voice and messaging
- Campaign creative and messaging strategy
- AEO/SEO content structure and wording
- Content output **feeds** distribution

**Content agent does NOT own**: WHERE or WHEN content is distributed. That is distribution.

---

## Distribution agent owns: WHERE and WHEN

- Distribution channels (X, email sends, paid placement, etc.)
- Posting schedule and timing
- Audience growth mechanics, platform strategy, channel testing
- Distribution timing optimization
- **Receives** content from content agent and optimizes distribution
- **Reports** distribution performance back to improve content

**Distribution agent does NOT own**: What the content says. That is content.

---
