# Example Short Mandates — Marketing Pod (Copy & Paste)

Use these in WhatsApp (or your chat interface) to get immediate wins with your Marketing-Lead. One line = one mandate.

---

## Performance brief

- **Run HEARTBEAT and send me this week’s performance brief.**
- **What’s our top-performing content in the last 7 days? What are you doing next?**
- **Send me metrics: engagement, clicks, conversions, and one thing you’re testing.**

---

## Content creation

- **Create and queue the next 5 posts for [channel].**
- **Draft the launch copy for [product/campaign] and hand off for distribution.**
- **Generate 3 new slogans and hooks for [offer] and log them with traceability.**

---

## Strategy & learning

- **Summarize what you learned from the last campaign and how you’re applying it.**
- **What’s one virality or conversion test you’re running this week?**
- **Give me one AEO/SEO or channel opportunity you’re pursuing.**

---

## One-off checks

- **Audit last week’s content for brand voice and conversion strength.**
- **What would you do differently if you had a dedicated distribution agent?**
- **Summarize marketing performance for the last 7 days in one brief.**

---

Short mandates only. The pod runs the rest.
