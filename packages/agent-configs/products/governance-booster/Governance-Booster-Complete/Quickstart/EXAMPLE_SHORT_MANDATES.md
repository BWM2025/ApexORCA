# Example Short Mandates — Governance Booster (Copy & Paste)

Use these in WhatsApp (or your chat interface) to get immediate wins with your Governance-Lead. One line = one mandate.

---

## Governance brief

- **Run HEARTBEAT and give me the governance brief.**
- **Audit all agents' last 24h and report compliance scores.**
- **Summarize top governance risks right now.**

---

## Compliance & veto

- **Which agents are below compliance threshold? What are you doing about it?**
- **List any actions you halted in the last 7 days and the outcome.**
- **Give me one recommendation to improve pod governance this week.**

---

## Research & upgrades

- **What’s one governance upgrade you’re proposing based on your research?**
- **Summarize regulatory or brand-sentiment risks you’re tracking.**
- **Are any agents idle or underperforming? What did you flag?**

---

## One-off checks

- **Full compliance snapshot: one line per agent.**
- **What would you veto right now if you had to choose one thing?**
- **Give me the nightly governance summary in 5 bullets.**

---

Short mandates only. Governance wraps; it doesn’t replace.
