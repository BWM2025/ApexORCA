# Example Short Mandates — Coordinating the Entire Pod

Copy, paste, and customize. Send to Pod-Orchestrator-Lead (or CEO-Lead) via WhatsApp or your chat interface.

---

## Status and alignment

- **Run cross-agent HEARTBEAT and send me the pod brief.**
- **Sync all agents and report alignment by EOD.**
- **Give me a one-page pod health summary for this week.**

---

## Focus and priorities

- **This week focus the pod on [launch / retention / content]. Confirm handoffs.**
- **Priority for the pod: [X]. Make sure Marketing, Technical, and Ops are aligned.**
- **All agents: align on [goal]. Report back with one sentence each.**

---

## Troubleshooting and review

- **Something feels off with handoffs. Run HEARTBEAT and flag any gaps.**
- **Review last 7 days of agent reports and tell me the top 3 risks.**
- **Ensure no one is stepping outside their lane. Use the charter and report.**

---

## One-time setup

- **Connect [Agent A] and [Agent B] to the pod. Confirm they’re in the shared memory and charter.**
- **Add [new role] to the pod charter. Update handoff rules and tell the pod.**

---

Keep it short. The pod is built to run; you only need to steer when you want to.
