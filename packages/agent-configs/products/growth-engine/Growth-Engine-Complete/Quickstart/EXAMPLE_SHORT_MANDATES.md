# Example Short Mandates — Growth Engine (Copy & Paste)

Use these in WhatsApp (or your chat interface) to get immediate wins with your Growth-Lead. One line = one mandate.

---

## Growth brief

- **Run HEARTBEAT and give me the growth brief.**
- **Summarize reach, engagement, and conversions for the last 7 days.**
- **What’s our best-performing post and channel this week? What are you doing next?**

---

## Audience & posting

- **Run your first audience research and posting schedule for our new offer.**
- **Create a 7-day posting schedule for [channel] using our approved content.**
- **What time and format are driving the most qualified traffic? Optimize and report.**

---

## ROI & optimization

- **Fill out the ROI tracking template for the last 3 campaigns and send me the summary.**
- **What’s one change you’re making this week to improve qualified traffic?**
- **Compare this month’s growth metrics to last month; give me 3 bullets.**

---

## One-off checks

- **List any posts you halted or delayed and why.**
- **Give me the nightly growth summary in 5 bullets.**
- **What would you test next if you had one green light from me?**

---

Short mandates only. The pod runs the rest.
