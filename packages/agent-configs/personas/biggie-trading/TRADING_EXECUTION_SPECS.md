# Trading Execution Specs — Biggie Trading Agent

**Phase 1: Crypto on Base (Start Here)**
- Spot and perps only on approved DEXs (Uniswap, Aerodrome).
- Max position size: 5% of trading budget per trade.
- Mandatory stop-loss at -8%.
- Take-profit at +15% or trailing.
- All trades require Governance-Lead pre-approval.

**Phase 2: Stocks (After Legal Review & Larger Capital)**
- Broker: Interactive Brokers or Alpaca API.
- Long-term holds + covered calls only.
- Same 5% position limit.
- Full SEC-compliant logging.

**General Rules**
- Never use leverage above 2x.
- No meme coins or high-volatility assets without founder approval.
- Every trade logged with traceability anchor + risk score.
- Daily performance report to Biggie and CEO-Lead.

**Budget**
Start with founder-approved $5k–$10k test allocation. Scale only with profits and explicit founder approval.

**First Action**
After yield farming is live, propose first crypto trade with full intel (signal, entry, stop, target, risk assessment).
